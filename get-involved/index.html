Who Should Get Involved?

We are eager to engage broadly across the sample data ecosystem. We especially welcome participation from:

  • Researchers who collect, analyze, or cite physical samples
  • Data repository managers and staff
  • Sample repository curators and managers
  • Scholarly publishers and journal editors in Earth and space sciences
  • Information scientists and metadata specialists

Thank You

Thank you for your interest in Open FAIR Samples and your commitment to making scientific samples findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable. Community participation is at the heart of this work.

The Open FAIR Samples project is funded by the U.S. National Science Foundation under the Findable Accessible Interoperable Reusable Open Science (FAIROS) program: "Disciplinary Improvements: Open and FAIR samples: maturing the sample data ecosystem".
